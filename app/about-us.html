The What Set Us Apart

What sets Fox & Firefly Photography apart from the other Twin Cities Engagement Photographers or Twin Cities Wedding Photographers out there:

  • Our post-processing of every photo is remarkably thorough. We spend a lot of time reviewing, retouching, color correcting, and highlighting the beautiful details of each and every photograph. We don't just select a small amount of photographs. We edit each photo that is delivered to you with an equal amount of care and thought.
  • When you meet with us, you meet the same photographers that you will be working with on your special day(s). There won’t be any wondering if the person you are communicating with and establishing a relationship with via phone or e-mail will be the actual person taking your photos. Sebastian and Danica will always be the only two people who will see everything through from start to finish.
  • We deliver your photos in three phases. In the First Phase, you will receive an e-mail with 1-3 high quality finished photographs in a week’s time, after your session (wedding or engagement) has concluded. In the Second Phase, two to three weeks after the day of your session, you will receive an e-mail that contains a larger “Sneak Peak” consisting of anywhere between 10-20+ images. At that time, your “Sneak Peak” will also be available on our Website and Facebook page. In the Third Phase, you will receive a Flash Drive consisting of all of your images fully edited and meticulously prepared just for you. This will also be the time when your password protected online gallery will be made available and any additions in your package will be delivered. This Third Phase will occur 4-6 weeks following Phase One, after you have reviewed your images.
  • We’re just as excited to see your photos as you are, so we work tirelessly to reap the rewards and share some of the excitement with you as we offer the two “Sneak Peaks” prior to the finished product being delivered. An additional phase is included for Wedding Packages Three and Four. In the Final Phase, after receiving and reviewing your images, we ask that you sit down with your partner and select the images which you would like to see included in your Wedding Album(s). We also ask that you make a note of which photos you would like enlarged. Please do keep in mind though, that the more images you include, the less space you will have on each page. After you have sent us your list, we will begin arranging the included photos into an album format. Upon the arrival of your Wedding Album(s), we will promptly deliver it/them to you.
