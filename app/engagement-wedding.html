Engagement & Wedding

Congratulations on your engagement! Now for the excitement of planning your wedding and settling on all the little details such as the: flowers, cake, dresses, food, wedding photographer-- just to name a few of the important parts of your special day. While you may not have it all figured out right now, everything will eventually come together in just the perfect way. Be assured, when you choose Fox & Firefly Photography to photograph your engagement and wedding, we will take the utmost care in preserving every precious moment surrounding one of the most important days of your lives.

Prices and Packages

NOTE: All Sessions include

  • 1 Photographer & 1 Assistant
  • A Flash Drive of 20+ High Resolution Retouched Digital Images (prints up to an 8x10 size)
  • Full Printing Rights

Engagement Sessions

Engagement Sessions include:

  • 60-180 Minute Session
  • Unlimited outfit changes within the allotted time
  • One location (a second location is allowed within 5 miles of the first location), within coverage area, unless otherwise noted.
  • Travel within 50 miles of the Twin Cities is included. Each additional 5 miles will be an added $5.
  • Available for travel outside of Minnesota.
  • Locations will either be outside (client or photographer’s choice) or in client’s home.

Session A (Independent of a Wedding Contract): $175

Session B (Booked with a Signed Wedding Contract): $50 for Wedding Session 1 and 2 (FREE for Wedding Sessions 3 and 4)

Wedding Sessions

Session 1: $500 (Booking Deposit: $100)

  • Photos After Ceremony Only: Immediate Family, Extended Family (Groom’s), Extended Family (Bride’s), and Bridal Party

Session 2: $1,000 (Booking Deposit: $300)

  • Photography of Wedding Day Events (not including reception); up to three locations.
  • $25 Credit toward any Future Photo Session

Session 3: $1,500 (Booking Deposit: $500)

  • FREE Engagement Session B
  • Photography of Wedding Day Events (including reception); up to three locations
  • Up to 8 Hours of Coverage with Unlimited Photos
  • Professioanl Flush Mount Album
  • $50 Credit Toward Any Future Photo Session

Session 4: $2,000 (Booking Deposit: $700)

  • FREE Engagement Session B
  • Photography of Wedding Day Events (including reception); up to three locations
  • Full Day Coverage with Unlimited Photos
  • Professioanl Flush Mount Album
  • $100 Credit Toward Any Future Session
  • 2 Parent Albums

NOTE: Rehearsal Dinner may be added to any session for an additional $200. Taxes Are Included in all Session Price.
